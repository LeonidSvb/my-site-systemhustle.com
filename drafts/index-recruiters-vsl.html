Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about how this works.

How do you identify companies that are about to hire?

+

I track multiple signals: funding rounds, leadership changes, LinkedIn activity patterns, job posting history, growth announcements. When a company shows several signals together, they're likely expanding their team soon - often before they've even posted a role publicly.

What kind of conversations should I expect?

+

These are warm intros, not cold leads. Expect 3-6 qualified intros per month depending on your niche. From every 5 intros, clients typically close 1-2+ placements. Results vary by recruiting specialty and market.

How is this different from other lead gen agencies?

+

Most agencies scrape job boards or blast generic messages to HR titles. I identify hiring intent BEFORE jobs are posted, so you're not competing with 50 other agencies for the same role. You get to the conversation first, with context.

How fast can we start?

+

48 hours from kickoff call. I map your ideal client profile, set up signal tracking for your niche, and start outreach within 2 business days. You'll see initial results within the first few weeks.

What if it doesn't work for my niche?

+

I work month-to-month after the initial period. If I'm not generating qualified conversations that make sense for your business, you can walk. No long-term contracts.
