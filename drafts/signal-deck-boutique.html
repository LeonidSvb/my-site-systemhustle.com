06 — If You Already Reach Out Yourself

Here's exactly how this fits in.

Depending on your niche, expect roughly 20–30 strong signals a month — up to 50 in a wider market. Each signal is a company, and inside many of them I find more than one real contact. About half come with a working email, and I reach out to those directly. The rest are LinkedIn-only — you send those yourself, but I tell you exactly what to write and when. That volume is what turns into the 3–8 real conversations a month on the cover — not every signal converts, but every one gets worked.

Replacing this yourself would mean running a small in-house research and BD team — most companies don't have 10–15 people free for that.
