Frequently Asked Questions

What if prospects don't respond to our messaging?

+

Generic messages get ignored. That's why we research each prospect's specific challenges first. When a CFO sees an email about the exact cash flow problem they complained about on LinkedIn last week, they respond. Our messaging gets significantly higher response rates than generic outreach.

How is this different from other agencies?

+

Most agencies blast thousands of generic messages hoping for minimal response. We send fewer, highly researched messages and get significantly better results. Quality over quantity. We target business problems, not job titles.

What if we can't handle too many meetings?

+

That's actually a good problem to have. We can throttle the system to match your capacity - fewer meetings for smaller teams, or ramp up for larger sales organizations. The system scales with your needs.

How does pricing work?

+

Flexible. There's a small setup fee to get everything configured and the first outreach running. After that, pricing is tied to results — you pay based on the meetings we book, not a flat retainer. No long-term contracts. Book a call and we'll put together something that makes sense for your situation.
