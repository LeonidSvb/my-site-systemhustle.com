We guarantee the agreed number of qualified conversations with decision-makers who have an active hiring need, confirmed budget, and authority to engage. If we miss the number by the end of the engagement — we continue at no additional cost until we hit it.
What "qualified intro" means
Intent
Actively hiring or planning to within 30–60 days. Not vague future interest.
Budget
Understands recruiter fees. Has budget allocated or confirmed funding to pay.
Authority
Owner, VP, or HR Director level. Not a coordinator or screener.
Urgency
A specific, confirmed hiring pain right now — not hypothetical. Signal proves it.
Interest
Has expressed willingness to speak. You arrive expected — not cold.
Fit
Company size, stage, and role type match your ICP. No mismatched conversations.
