What I do

I monitor the layer before the job post.
Then I bring you in first.

Not job boards. Not LinkedIn. The signals that show a company is about to need a hire — weeks before they call anyone.

Leadership changes — new executives, department head departure, management restructure. Hiring needs follow within weeks.
Regulatory and licensing signals — filings, expansions, compliance changes that precede staffing needs by 3–6 weeks.
Growth and contract signals — funding rounds, new contracts, M&A activity. All visible before anyone posts a job.

I score these, stack them by urgency, and introduce you at the right moment — as a referral, not a cold call.
